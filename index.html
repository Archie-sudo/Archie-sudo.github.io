About the Book

Live or Die. Eat or Get Eaten: The Brutal Reality of Life dissects the instincts, systems, and stories that shape how we struggle, survive, and seek meaning. It’s sharp, unsettling, and strangely hopeful.

  • Core themes: survival, power, agency, moral ambiguity
  • For readers who like: raw honesty, philosophy, and modern critique
  • Format: Paperback & eBook
